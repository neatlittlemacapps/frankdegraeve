Unified international multi-brand, multi-product online platform for one of the world's largest chocolate manufacturers.

The challenge was consolidating up to 20 different brands serving different regions and industries into one coherent website — separating product offerings and content for three core industries without mixing audiences.

I created a smart information architecture that logically divided products and content by industry while giving audiences clear pathways to explore other segments. I co-created and validated a prototype through user testing.
