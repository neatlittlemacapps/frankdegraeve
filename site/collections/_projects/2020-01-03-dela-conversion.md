Fundamentally rethought the online insurance quote intake flow to remove barriers and dramatically improve conversion.

The existing form had a low conversion rate due to cognitive overload. I conducted user research and stakeholder workshops to redesign the flow — increasing the number of steps while drastically reducing cognitive load per step. I introduced motion design to create a perceived sense of speed.

**Results:** 300% conversion increase on mobile, 211% on desktop — far exceeding the initial 30% estimate.
