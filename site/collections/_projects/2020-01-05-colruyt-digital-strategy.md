Defined a new digital communication strategy for a complex ecosystem of over 40 brands.

I facilitated the definition of a new vision, mission, and strategic goals using the **LEGO Serious Play** facilitation method (for which I hold certification). The result was a set of simple guiding principles and a framework for continuous adaptation.

The framework is not just a document — it's a functional tool that lives in day-to-day work, enabling employees to decide what content to create, for whom, and where it belongs in the ecosystem.
