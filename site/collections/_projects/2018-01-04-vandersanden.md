New international web platform for a leading brick manufacturer, catering to architects, engineers, and end-customers across multiple markets.

I ran on-site workshops and customer interviews to understand the business and customer segments. Through customer journey mapping, service blueprinting, and a Jobs to Be Done workshop I defined and prioritised solutions — then validated them through user testing on prototypes.

The resulting architecture is intuitively navigable for all segments, with interconnected content that enables a strong ongoing content strategy.
