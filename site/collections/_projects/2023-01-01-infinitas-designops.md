How do you raise design maturity across multiple legacy brands without breaking what's already working?

Infinitas Learning — born from merging several educational publishers — had multiple live platforms in active development but no shared operations connecting design, research, and creative work across them.

I built and scaled a shared design system across educational platforms in a multi-brand, multi-audience domain. I introduced DesignOps practices to unify design processes across product, brand, and content teams, and aligned design maturity efforts across multiple European markets.
