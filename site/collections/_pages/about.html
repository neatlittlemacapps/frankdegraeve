I'm Frank De Graeve — a designer and design operations leader based in Belgium. My work sits at the intersection of DesignOps, UX strategy, and AI adoption.

A lot of what I do is invisible: building the scaffolding that lets design teams work at scale, shaping how organisations think about design maturity, and helping teams build real AI fluency rather than surface-level tool familiarity.

I build systems, not screens.

I currently lead design operations at Infinitas Learning, where I govern and build out a cross-brand, cross-product, AI-integrated design system spanning multiple European educational publishers. I also teach design operations and service design as an instructor at T-shaped Academy.

Previously I've worked as a UX and service design consultant at The Reference, a UX designer at Plantyn, and a digital strategist at Colruyt Group.
